About Me

Alec Kosla is a recent graduate of Ball State University, and a current student in the University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. Alec's interest in coding began when some of his friends began to code and he found their job to be much more fulfilling then his current career. He is now a passionate student of the bootcamp, spending many hours practicing coding and doing his best to learn and becomes successful in his new field.
